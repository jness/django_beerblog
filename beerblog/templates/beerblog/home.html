Welcome To Nessy's Tavern

About The Bar:

Nessy's Tavern was constructed in 2010 in Round Rock Texas. As a project designed to pass some time, a mere two weeks later the Tavern was completed. The bar comes equipped with a nine holding wine rack, and shelving for glassware. A keg-o-rator added to the mix, and we had a fully functional bar.

The design and build process is documented via photograph here.
